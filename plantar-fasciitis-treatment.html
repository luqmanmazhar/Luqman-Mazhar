Plantar Fasciitis FAQs

Should I keep walking with plantar fasciitis?

Usually yes, but activity may need modifying depending on your symptoms.
Can I still run?

Often yes—but mileage may need adjusting temporarily.
How long does plantar fasciitis take to recover?

Recovery varies, but most people improve faster with proper rehab.
