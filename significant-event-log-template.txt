PURPOSE:
This log records any significant events, near misses, complaints or incidents
that occur in clinical practice. Recording and reviewing events is a core part
of clinical governance and HCPC audit readiness.

WHAT TO RECORD:
- Clinical errors or near misses
- Adverse reactions to treatment
- Patient falls or accidents on premises
- Complaints received (formal or informal)
- Safeguarding concerns
- Data breaches or confidentiality incidents
- Equipment failures affecting patient safety
