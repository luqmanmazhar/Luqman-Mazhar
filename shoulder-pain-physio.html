Common Causes of Shoulder Pain

Shoulder pain can develop from gym injuries, poor posture, repetitive work, sports injuries or sudden trauma.

Shoulder pain may also be linked to neck pain or sports injuries. You may also benefit from our neck pain treatment or sports injury rehab.

Shoulder Pain FAQs

Can physio help frozen shoulder?

Yes — physiotherapy can help improve movement and reduce stiffness over time.
Why does my shoulder hurt when lifting weights?

This may be caused by overload, poor mechanics or rotator cuff irritation.
Why can’t I lift my arm properly?

This could be linked to impingement, rotator cuff issues or frozen shoulder.
Can I train through shoulder pain?

Sometimes yes — but exercises often need modifying while healing.
