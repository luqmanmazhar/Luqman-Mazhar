Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Primus Physio in Stoke-on-Trent? +
Primus Physio is based at Copthall House, King Street, Newcastle-under-Lyme — approximately 10 minutes from Stoke-on-Trent city centre by car. Free parking is available outside the building.
How far is it from Stoke to Primus Physio? +
Approximately 10 minutes from Hanley city centre via the A34 or A500. Newcastle-under-Lyme is adjacent to Stoke-on-Trent and easily accessible from Hanley, Burslem, Tunstall, Longton and Fenton.
Do I need a GP referral? +
No — you can book directly without a GP referral and be seen the same week. NHS physiotherapy waiting lists in Staffordshire are typically 6–18 weeks; private appointments are often available within days.
Do you accept health insurance for Stoke-on-Trent patients? +
Yes — Primus Physio is recognised by AXA Health, Bupa, WPA, Aviva and Vitality Health. Insured patients from across Stoke-on-Trent are welcome. Contact your insurer to get pre-authorisation before your first session.
How much does a session cost? +
The first session is £60 (60 minutes) — including full assessment, diagnosis and hands-on treatment. Follow-up sessions are £40 (30 minutes). No GP referral needed.
Is there parking at the clinic? +
Yes — free on-street parking is available directly outside Copthall House on King Street, subject to availability. Several free and low-cost car parks are also within easy walking distance in Newcastle-under-Lyme town centre.
