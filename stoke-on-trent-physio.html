Questions

Questions from Stoke Patients

How far is the clinic from Stoke-on-Trent? +
The clinic is based in Newcastle-under-Lyme, approximately 10 minutes from Hanley (Stoke city centre) by car. Most Stoke areas are within a 10–18 minute drive, and the journey is straightforward via the A34 or A500. Free parking is available at and near the clinic.
Is there parking at the clinic? +
Yes — free parking is available at and near the clinic. This is a real advantage over Stoke city centre clinics where patients often have to pay for parking and walk to the appointment.
Can I use private health insurance from Stoke? +
Yes — Primus Physio is a recognised provider for AXA Health, Bupa, WPA, Aviva and Vitality Health. Patients from Stoke-on-Trent and across Staffordshire can use their private health insurance. Bring your membership number and authorisation code to your appointment.
Why travel to Newcastle-under-Lyme for physio rather than staying in Stoke? +
Many Stoke patients choose Primus Physio for the combination of elite sport experience (Port Vale FC Academy), genuinely 1-to-1 sessions, same-week availability and free parking. The 10-minute journey from most of Stoke is comparable to travelling across the city to a clinic in Stoke itself.
Do you offer evening and weekend appointments for Stoke patients? +
Yes — appointments are available Monday to Friday 8am–8pm and Saturday to Sunday 9am–4pm, making it easy for Stoke patients to book around work commitments.
Do I need a GP referral? +
No — you can book directly without a GP referral and be seen the same week. No NHS waiting list, no referral letter needed.
