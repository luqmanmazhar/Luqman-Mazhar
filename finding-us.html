Look for Copthall House in large letters above the main double-door entrance on King Street. Once you've found the building, follow the instructions below depending on your appointment time.

Arrival instructions

When You Arrive

Follow the steps below depending on your appointment time.

Main entrance — Copthall House, King Street, Newcastle-under-Lyme

Weekdays before 5pm

Daytime Appointments

  1. Enter through the main double doors on King Street
  2. Speak to the receptionist and let them know you have a Primus Physio appointment
  3. Take a seat in the waiting area
  4. Luqman will come and collect you shortly
After 5pm & Weekends

Evening & Weekend Appointments

  1. Wait outside the main entrance doors on King Street
  2. Text 07867 782786 to let Luqman know you've arrived
  3. Luqman will come down and collect you from the entrance
Getting here

Parking & Distance

Free Parking

Free parking is available directly outside and around Copthall House on King Street, subject to availability. Several free and low-cost car parks are also within easy walking distance in Newcastle-under-Lyme town centre.

🚗

From Stoke-on-Trent

Approximately 10 minutes from Hanley city centre by car. Newcastle-under-Lyme is easily accessible via the A34 and A500. King Street runs through the town centre.
