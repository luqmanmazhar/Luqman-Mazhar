Getting here

Finding the Clinic

The clinic is based inside Copthall House, King Street, Newcastle-under-Lyme, ST5 1EL — a large office building on King Street in the town centre. Look for "Copthall House" in large letters above the main double-door entrance.

Main entrance — Copthall House, King Street

🕔 Weekdays before 5pm

  1. Enter through the main double doors on King Street
  2. Speak to the receptionist and let them know you have a Primus Physio appointment
  3. Take a seat in the waiting area — Luqman will come and collect you

🌙 After 5pm & Weekends

  1. Wait outside the main entrance doors on King Street
  2. Text 07867 782786 to let Luqman know you've arrived
  3. Luqman will come down and collect you from the entrance

🅿️ Parking

Free parking is available directly outside and around Copthall House on King Street, subject to availability. There are also several free and low-cost car parks within easy walking distance in Newcastle-under-Lyme town centre.
