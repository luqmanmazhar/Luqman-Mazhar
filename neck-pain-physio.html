Common Causes of Neck Pain

Neck pain can be caused by poor posture, desk work, sleeping positions, stress-related tension, gym injuries or whiplash injuries.

Neck pain can also contribute to shoulder pain and headaches. You may also benefit from our shoulder pain treatment or sports injury rehab.

Neck Pain FAQs

Why is my neck pain causing headaches?

Tension in the neck muscles and joints can commonly trigger headaches.
Can physio help trapped nerves in the neck?

Yes — physiotherapy can reduce irritation and improve mobility.
Can gym training cause neck pain?

Yes — poor lifting technique and upper body overload can trigger symptoms.
How long does neck pain take to recover?

Many patients improve within a few sessions depending on severity.
