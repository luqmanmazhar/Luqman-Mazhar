Common Causes of Neck Pain

Neck pain can be caused by poor posture, desk work, gym injuries, sleeping positions, stress-related tension or whiplash injuries.

Neck pain can also contribute to shoulder pain and headaches. You may also benefit from our shoulder pain treatment or sports injury rehab.

How Primus Physio Can Help

We assess the root cause of your neck pain and create a personalised treatment plan to help you recover quickly and prevent future flare-ups.

  • Manual therapy
  • Mobility exercises
  • Strength rehabilitation
  • Posture correction
  • Return to gym/sport plans
