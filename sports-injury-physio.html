Questions

Sports Rehab FAQs

Can you help me return to sport quickly? +
Yes — rehabilitation is built around safe, evidence-based return-to-play timelines. The goal is always to get you back as soon as it's safe to do so, without cutting corners that risk re-injury.
Do you help runners with injuries? +
Yes — we regularly treat running injuries including shin splints, IT band syndrome, plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendinopathy and knee pain, with structured return-to-running programmes.
Can you rehab gym and lifting injuries? +
Yes — we help gym members recover from muscle strains, tendon injuries and overuse problems, with rehabilitation designed to get you back to lifting confidently.
Do you work with non-professional athletes? +
Absolutely. The same high-performance rehab principles used in professional sport are applied to every patient — from complete beginners to competitive athletes.
Do I need a GP referral for sports rehabilitation? +
No — you can book directly without a GP referral. Most athletes choose to get started straight away rather than wait for a GP appointment.
