Common Causes of Knee Pain

Knee pain can develop from running injuries, sports injuries, gym training, poor movement mechanics, arthritis or sudden trauma.

Knee pain may also be linked to lower back issues or sports injuries. You may also benefit from our back pain treatment or sports rehab.

How We Treat Knee Pain

We assess the root cause of your knee pain and build a treatment plan to reduce pain, improve movement and help you return to normal activity.

  • Manual therapy
  • Strength rehabilitation
  • Running rehab plans
  • Gym rehab plans
  • Mobility work
  • Return to sport planning
