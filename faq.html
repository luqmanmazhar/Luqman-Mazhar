Common Physiotherapy Questions

What conditions do you treat? +
We treat a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions including back pain, neck pain, sports injuries, knee pain, shoulder pain and sciatica.

This includes problems such as disc injuries, muscle strains, ligament sprains, tendon pain and post-surgical rehabilitation.

We regularly help patients across Stoke-on-Trent and Newcastle-under-Lyme recover from pain and return to normal activity.
How quickly can I get an appointment? +
We offer fast access to physiotherapy in Stoke-on-Trent and Newcastle-under-Lyme, often with same-week appointments available including evenings and weekends.

Early treatment helps reduce pain and speeds up recovery.
Do I need a GP referral? +
No — you can book directly without needing a GP referral.

Private physiotherapy allows you to access treatment quickly without long NHS waiting times.
What happens in the first session? +
Your first session includes a full assessment, clear diagnosis, hands-on treatment and a personalised rehabilitation plan.

We focus on identifying the root cause of your pain and giving you a clear plan so you know exactly how to recover.
How much does physiotherapy cost? +
Initial Assessment: £60 – Includes full assessment, diagnosis, treatment and a structured rehabilitation plan.
Follow-Up: £40
Extended Follow-Up: £50

We focus on getting results efficiently, meaning fewer sessions and better long-term outcomes.

You can view full details on pricing and all available treatments on our physiotherapy services and pricing page.
How many sessions will I need? +
This depends on your condition, but many patients begin to see improvement within just a few sessions.

Acute injuries may resolve quickly, while longer-term problems may require a structured rehabilitation programme.

We always aim to get you better as quickly and effectively as possible.
