Common Sciatica Symptoms

Pain travelling from lower back into the leg
Burning or sharp nerve pain
Numbness or tingling
Pain when sitting for long periods
Weakness in the leg
Pain when bending or lifting

Common Causes of Sciatica

Sciatica often happens due to irritation of the sciatic nerve.

  • Disc injuries
  • Lower back stiffness
  • Poor lifting technique
  • Gym injuries
  • Long periods of sitting
  • Nerve irritation

You may also benefit from our back pain treatment or sports injury rehab.

Sciatica FAQs

Can physio help trapped nerves?

Yes — physiotherapy can help reduce nerve irritation and address the root cause.
Why does my leg feel numb or tingly?

Sciatica can irritate the sciatic nerve and cause numbness or tingling.
Should I rest with sciatica?

Complete rest usually isn't recommended. The right rehab plan helps recovery.
When should I seek treatment for sciatica?

If pain travels down your leg or affects daily life, get assessed early.
