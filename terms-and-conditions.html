4. Cancellation Policy

We require at least 24 hours notice to cancel or rearrange an appointment. This allows us to offer the appointment to another patient who may be waiting.

  • More than 24 hours notice: No charge. You may rebook at no cost.
  • Less than 24 hours notice: A cancellation fee of 50% of the session fee may be charged.
  • No show (no contact): The full session fee will be charged.
  • Repeated late cancellations: We reserve the right to require payment in advance for future bookings.

We understand that emergencies and genuine illness occur. These will be considered on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel.

If we need to cancel your appointment, we will give you as much notice as possible and offer to rebook at no additional cost.
