Why pain persists

Why Your Pain Keeps Coming Back

If pain returns despite rest or previous treatment, the root cause likely hasn't been addressed.

Reducing activity when in pain feels right — but it leads to stiffness, weakness and worsening symptoms over time.

Pain settles briefly, then returns the moment you try to get back to normal life. Breaking that cycle requires treating the cause, not just the symptoms.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a GP referral? +
No — you can book directly without a referral. Most patients choose to start treatment straight away without waiting for GP appointments.
How quickly can I get an appointment? +
Appointments are often available within the same week, including evenings and weekends.
What happens in the first session? +
Your first session includes a full assessment, diagnosis and initial treatment. You'll leave with a clear plan explaining what's causing your pain and how to fix it.
Do you offer hands-on treatment? +
Yes — treatment sessions include hands-on techniques to reduce pain and improve movement, alongside a personalised rehabilitation plan.
Can I use my private health insurance? +
Yes. We are registered with AXA Health, Bupa, WPA, Aviva, and Vitality. Simply bring your membership details and authorisation code to your appointment.
Do you treat runners and gym injuries? +
Yes — we regularly treat running injuries, gym-related pain and sports injuries, helping you return to activity safely and confidently.
Do you offer weekend appointments? +
Yes — flexible appointments are available including evenings and weekends.
