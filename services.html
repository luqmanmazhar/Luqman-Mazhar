Every session

What Your Treatment May Include

A structured approach combining hands-on treatment and rehabilitation to reduce pain and address the root cause.

1

Detailed Movement Assessment

We assess how your body moves to identify the underlying cause of your pain — not just where it hurts.

2

Hands-On Treatment

Manual therapy techniques to reduce pain, improve mobility and help you move more comfortably.

3

Personalised Rehab Plan

A tailored programme designed to correct the root cause and build long-term strength and resilience.

4

Return to Activity

Your plan is progressed over time to help you return confidently to normal activity without setbacks.
